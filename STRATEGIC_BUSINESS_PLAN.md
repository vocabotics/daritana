# DARITANA ARCHITECT PLATFORM
## Comprehensive Business Model & Strategic Plan for Malaysian Market

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Daritana Architect Platform represents a revolutionary B2B2C digital ecosystem designed to transform Malaysia's RM 25 billion architecture and interior design industry. By combining project management, regulatory compliance, design collaboration, and marketplace features into a unified platform, Daritana addresses critical inefficiencies that cost the industry an estimated RM 3.75 billion annually in productivity losses.

### Key Investment Highlights
- **Market Opportunity**: RM 25 billion Malaysian AEC market growing at 8.5% CAGR
- **Target Market**: 15,000+ registered architects, 25,000+ interior designers, 50,000+ contractors
- **Revenue Model**: Multi-stream SaaS + marketplace with 35% gross margins by Year 3
- **Projected Year 5 Revenue**: RM 125 million with EBITDA margins of 28%
- **Strategic Moat**: First-mover advantage with regulatory body partnerships (PAM, BEM, MSID)
